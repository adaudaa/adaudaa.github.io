Website Evaluations

Yale School of Art

The Yale School of Art website showcases information about their art programs, faculty, and events.

  • Contrast: The website lacks sufficient contrast between the background and text colors, making it difficult to read the content.
  • Repetition: There is a lack of consistent repetition of design elements throughout the website, resulting in a disjointed appearance.
  • Alignment: The alignment of elements on the website is inconsistent, causing a cluttered and unorganized look.
  • Proximity: The spacing and grouping of related elements are not well-defined, making it challenging to navigate and find information.

Jasper.ai

Jasper.ai is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps users create high-quality content quickly and easily.

  • Contrast: The website uses a clean and contrasting color scheme, making the content easy to read and visually appealing.
  • Repetition: The website maintains a consistent design style throughout, with repeated elements such as fonts, colors, and buttons, creating a cohesive user experience.
  • Alignment: The layout of the website is well-aligned, with elements placed in a structured and organized manner, enhancing readability and visual appeal.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped together and have appropriate spacing, making it easy for users to navigate and find the information they need.
