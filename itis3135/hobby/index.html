How to Play

Prompt: Generate an infographic-style image illustrating the basic rules of soccer, including the number of players, objective, and key guidelines.

Soccer is a simple yet captivating game. The objective is to score more goals than the opposing team by kicking the ball into their goal.

Each team consists of 11 players on the field, including a goalkeeper who is responsible for preventing the ball from entering their team's goal.

Players advance the ball up the field by dribbling, passing, and ultimately attempting to score. Fouls, such as tripping or pushing, result in penalties or free kicks for the opposing team.
